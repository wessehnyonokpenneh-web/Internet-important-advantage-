Modern Learning System

The internet has changed education completely by introducing digital learning. Students can now attend online classes, watch educational videos, and read digital textbooks from anywhere in the world.

This system makes learning more flexible and allows students to study at their own pace, improving understanding and performance.

Online Resources

Educational platforms provide tutorials, exercises, and exams that help students practice and improve their knowledge. Websites like Khan Academy and Coursera are widely used globally.
